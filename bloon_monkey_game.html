How to play
Move with WASD or arrow keys. Aim with your mouse / touch. Hold mouse to auto-fire darts. Kill bloons to gain XP — leveling improves HP, damage, and fire-rate. Max level is 20 (at level 20 you become extremely strong: triple damage and pierce on darts, and max HP scales to 2000).
Notes: Bloons get stronger each wave (more HP, more damage). If you die press R to restart.
Tip: Survive early waves and level quickly to handle later waves — max level makes you very powerful.
