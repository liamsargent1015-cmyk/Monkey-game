# 🐵 MONKEYS - Bloon Slayer 3D

A fun and engaging 3D browser-based game where you play as a powerful monkey defending against waves of colorful bloons!

## 🎮 Game Features

### Gameplay
- **3D Third-Person Combat**: Walk around freely and attack incoming bloons
- **80 Rounds of Action**: Progressively harder waves with increasingly powerful bloons
- **10 Level Progression System**: Unlock new weapons and abilities as you level up
- **Two Maps**: Choose between Beach or Grassland environments
- **Health & Leveling System**: Track your HP, kills, and experience points

### Level Progression

| Level | Weapon | HP | Damage | Special Effect |
|-------|--------|----|---------| -------|
| 1 | Fist | 100 | 10 | Basic punching |
| 2 | Bat | 120 | 20 | Knockback (8) |
| 3 | Spike | 125 | 35 | Pierce damage |
| 4 | Taco | 200 | 40 | Fire damage (sauce) |
| 5 | Boulder | 150 | 50 | Splash damage |
| 6 | Lightsaber | 200 | 80 | Energy slice |
| 7 | Mech Suit | 700 | 100 | Area stomp |
| 8 | Fist (8x Speed) | 400 | 10 | 8x attack speed |
| 9 | Nerf Gun | 1000 | 150 | Blast projectiles |
| 10 | Godzilla Breath | 2000 | 200 | Laser breath & stomp |

### Special Bloons
- **Round 40**: Big Bloon with Arms (100 HP, 40 damage to you)
- **Round 80**: Rainbow Bloon (200 HP, 80 damage to you) - The final challenge!

## 🎮 Controls

- **W/A/S/D** - Move forward/left/backward/right
- **Mouse Move** - Look around
- **Left Click** - Attack bloons
- **Space** - Jump (coming soon)

## 🗺️ Maps

### Beach
Sunny beach environment perfect for a relaxing battle. Features palm trees and sandy terrain.

### Grassland
Lush green grassland with trees. Great for stealth and strategy.

## 🎯 Game Mechanics

### Experience & Leveling
- Gain XP by defeating bloons
- Each level requires more XP to reach
- Unlock powerful new weapons and stat boosts with each level

### Difficulty Scaling
- Bloons get faster and healthier each round
- Spawn rate increases as rounds progress
- Boss bloons appear at key milestones (Round 40 & 80)

### Combat
- Click to attack bloons within range
- Different weapons have different effects:
  - Some provide knockback
  - Some deal area damage
  - Some have special effects like fire damage
- Your level and weapon determine your damage output and attack speed

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Click on your preferred map (Beach or Grassland)
3. Use WASD to move and click to attack bloons
4. Defeat bloons to gain XP and level up
5. Survive all 80 rounds to win!

## 📊 Scoring

- Each bloon defeated grants XP and increases your kill count
- Early rounds are easier with weaker bloons
- Later rounds feature stronger, faster bloons with higher HP
- Special boss bloons grant massive XP rewards

## 🎨 Technical Stack

- **Three.js**: 3D graphics rendering
- **WebGL**: Hardware-accelerated graphics
- **Vanilla JavaScript**: Game logic and mechanics
- **HTML5/CSS3**: UI and styling

## 💡 Tips & Tricks

1. **Level up quickly early**: Farm XP in the first few rounds before bloons get too strong
2. **Keep moving**: Don't let bloons corner you
3. **Use knockback**: Higher level weapons can push bloons away
4. **Watch your HP**: Manage your health carefully, especially against boss bloons
5. **Learn the maps**: Know where to position yourself for maximum defense

## 🏆 Victory Conditions

Beat all 80 rounds and defeat the final Rainbow Bloon to achieve victory!

## 📝 Game Balance

The game is designed to progressively challenge the player:
- Each weapon unlock provides meaningful gameplay improvements
- Boss rounds at 40 and 80 present major difficulty spikes
- The health scaling keeps the game challenging while remaining fair
- Attack speed multipliers at high levels create powerful late-game abilities
